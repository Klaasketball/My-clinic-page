About the Event

The Amstelveen Coaching Clinic is a three-day event featuring basketball clinics and a tournament. The event will host well-known international FIBA coaches. On Friday, there will be a full day of basketball clinics for players and coaches. Saturday will feature a basketball tournament with a variety of international teams, and Sunday will host the finals of the tournament. Sleeping and eating arrangements will be provided.

Clinic Program

Friday from 11:00 until 18:00, clinics will be given by 5 FIBA coaches to age groups 14 to 16 and 16 to 19 for both boys and girls.

From 18:00 to 20:30, there will be a coaching clinic for coaches who coach under 16 players.

From 20:30 to 22:30, there will be a coaching clinic for coaches who coach under 19 players. Coaches admission tickets give entry to both clinics.

Tournament

The tournament is for boys under 19 years, born in or after 2006. There are two groups (poules) of three teams each. The number 1 of poule A will play against the number 2 of poule B, and the number 2 of poule A will play the number 1 of poule B in the semi-finals. The winners of the semi-finals will play in the finals on Sunday. Teams are from Spain, Croatia, Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany.
